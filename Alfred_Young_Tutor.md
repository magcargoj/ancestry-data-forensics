## Biography

Alfred Young Tutor was born on June 3, 1852, in North Carolina. He was the son of William Madison Tutor (born ~1807) and Harriet Ligon. He married Charlotte "Lottie" Honeycutt. He appears in the 1910 and 1920 censuses in Harnett County. He passed away on May 18, 1940, and is buried in the Tutor Family Cemetery.

Note: Some earlier records mistakenly attributed his father's birth year (1807) to him.
