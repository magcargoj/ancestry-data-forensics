## Biography

Calvin D. Brooks was a farmer from the North Fork district of Ashe County, NC. Following the outbreak of the Civil War, he enlisted as a Private on August 27, 1861, in the **37th North Carolina Infantry, Company A** (the "Ashe Beaver Creek Guards").

His service was tragically short. His regiment moved into Tennessee in early 1862, where they suffered heavily from disease. Calvin died in a Confederate hospital in **Knoxville, Tennessee**, on September 9, 1862. Like many soldiers who died in hospital camps, he was likely buried in a local Confederate cemetery, such as Bethel Cemetery in Knoxville.
