## Biography

Clyde Jones Jr. was born in 1914 in Avery County, NC. He was a veteran of the US Army and served for 68 years alongside his wife, Rose Mary Jones. He was the retired manager of the Johnson City Elks Club and worked at Nance Lanes and GPS Gas Station. He passed away in 2011 at the James H. Quillen VA Medical Center.
