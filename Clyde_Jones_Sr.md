## Biography

Clyde Jones Sr. was born in October 1887 in North Carolina. He was the son of **Henry Raymond Jones** and **Charlotte Deane Vance**. He worked as a manager for the Linville River Railroad Company. He married Maude Elma Matney on August 19, 1911, in Avery County, NC. He passed away on June 14, 1962, in Johnson City, Tennessee.
