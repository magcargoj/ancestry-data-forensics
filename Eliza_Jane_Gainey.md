Eliza Jane Gainey was born on December 20, 1820, in Roseboro, Sampson County, North Carolina. She married Marshall Andrew Culbreth Sr. and they were the parents of Margaret Ann "Maggie" Culbreth. Eliza passed away in March 1888 in Roseboro.
