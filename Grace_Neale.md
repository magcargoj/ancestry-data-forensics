## Biography

Grace Neale immigrated to Virginia in 1618. She was married to Edward Waters, a survivor of the *Sea Venture* shipwreck in Bermuda. Following Edward's death in 1630, she married Colonel [[Obedience_Robins]]. This second marriage effectively merged two prominent Eastern Shore families. Her son, [[William_Waters_Sr]], later married her stepdaughter, [[Margaret_Robins_Clark]].

In older genealogical legends, she was sometimes referred to as "Grace O'Neil" and linked to Irish nobility, but modern research confirms her origins in the Neale family of Northamptonshire, England.
