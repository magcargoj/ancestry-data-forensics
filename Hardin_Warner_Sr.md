## Biography

Hardin Warner Sr. was a Private in the 5th North Carolina Regiment of the Continental Line during the Revolutionary War. He enlisted on October 1, 1779, in Greene (then Dobbs) County and served for the duration of the war. His service is documented in pension file W18253, which includes affidavits from his children describing the hardships the family faced while he was in service. He is buried in the Warner Family Cemetery in Moore County.
