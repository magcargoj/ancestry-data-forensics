## Biography

Henry Munro Loudermilk (also spelled Lowdermilk) was a Confederate veteran of the Civil War. He enlisted on July 14, 1862, in Caldwell County, NC, in Company F of the 58th North Carolina Infantry (the "Caldwell Guards"). His regiment fought in the Western Theater, including the Battle of Chickamauga and the Atlanta Campaign, eventually surrendering at Bennett Place in 1865.
