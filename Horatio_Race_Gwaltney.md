## Biography

Horatio "Race" Gwaltney was born in 1791 in Craven County, North Carolina, the son of Benjamin Gwaltney Sr. and Rachel Jarrell Garrell. He married Barbara Pearce and they were the parents of Mary Ann Gwaltney. Horatio passed away before 1860 in North Carolina.
