## Biography

Jefferson Davis Medlin was born in 1870 in Union County, North Carolina. He was the son of **Ervin Medlin** and **Lydia P. Muse**. He married Georgia Lee Grimes in 1890. He moved to the High Point area later in life and passed away in Statesville, NC, in 1962.
