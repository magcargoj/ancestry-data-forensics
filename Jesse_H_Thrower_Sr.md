## Biography

Jesse H. Thrower Sr. was a veteran of the **War of 1812**. He was born in Halifax County, NC, in 1767 and died in Chatham County in 1831. His family’s migration from Halifax to Chatham and then to the Moore/Montgomery area is consistent with the movement of mixed-heritage communities in early North Carolina.
