## Biography

John Allen Shipes Sr. (born John Thomas Shipes) was born in 1900 in South Carolina. He was the son of **John Thomas Shipes** and **Lillie Mae Tuten**. He lived in Charlotte, NC, for many years and worked in the textile industry. He married Anna Lee Nichols.
