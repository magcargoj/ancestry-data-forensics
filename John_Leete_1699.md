## Biography

John Leete was born in 1699 in Guilford, Connecticut. He married Elizabeth Baldwin in 1724. John was the great-grandson of **Governor William Leete** (1612–1683), a prominent colonial figure who served as Governor of both the New Haven and Connecticut Colonies.
