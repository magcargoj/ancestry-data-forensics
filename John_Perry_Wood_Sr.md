## Biography

John Perry Wood Sr. was born in 1906 and lived in North Carolina. He was the son of John Riley Wood and Margaret Delaney Champion (who were uncle and niece). He married Magnolia "Maggie" Arnold on July 4, 1929. He passed away at his home in Olivia, NC, on February 28, 1955, and is buried in the Wood Family Cemetery.
