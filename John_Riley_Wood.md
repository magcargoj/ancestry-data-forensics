## Biography

John Riley Wood was born on July 31, 1870, in Harnett County, North Carolina. He was the son of Mark John Wood and Rebecca Smith Wood. Around 1897, he married Margaret Delaney Champion, who was his niece (the daughter of his sister Mary Jane Wood). They had a large family, including John Perry Wood Sr.

In the 1910 and 1920 censuses, John Riley and Margaret are listed in Harnett County with their children. John was a resident of Harnett County for his entire life. He passed away in 1946 and is buried in the Wood Family Cemetery.
