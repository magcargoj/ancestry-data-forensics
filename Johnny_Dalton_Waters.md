## Biography

**Note: This record is currently under investigation for historical accuracy.**

Johnny Dalton Waters is traditionally listed as being born in Bulloch, Georgia, in 1700. This is historically anachronistic as the Georgia colony was not founded until 1733. It is more likely he was born in North Carolina or Virginia and migrated to Georgia during the colonial period. He is a primary ancestor for many Waters families in Bulloch County, GA.
