## Biography

Juliana (Julia) Szeles was born in December 1868 in Debraď, Moldava nad Bodvou, Slovakia. She was the daughter of Emericus (James) Szeles and Anna Molnár. She married Sandor (Alexander) Szeles and they were the parents of Rozalia (Rose) Szelesova.
