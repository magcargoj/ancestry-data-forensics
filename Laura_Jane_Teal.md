## Biography

Laura Jane Teal was born on May 22, 1858, in North Carolina. She is identified in the 1860 Wake County Census as the daughter of **William Honeycutt** and **Martha**. It is possible she was later adopted or used the surname "Teal." She married Alfred Dawson Dockery Warner around 1878. She passed away in Charlotte, NC, in 1930.
