## Biography

Lizzie Briley Brown was born around 1896 in Pitt County, North Carolina. She was the daughter of Jesse John Holland Briley and Mississippi Carson. She married Jessie M. Brown and they resided in Edgecombe County. She is a key link between the Briley and Brown families of the NC Coastal Plain.
