## Biography

Lonnie Frank Arnold Sr. was born on April 2, 1866, in North Carolina. He was the son of Thomas Harvill Arnold and Elizabeth "Betsy" Rebecca Newell. He married Magnolia Catherine Tutor and they raised a large family in Buckhorn Township, Harnett County. He is listed in the 1910, 1920, and 1930 censuses as a farmer. He passed away on March 22, 1948, and is buried in the Baptist Grove Baptist Church Cemetery.
