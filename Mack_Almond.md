## Biography

Mack Monroe Almond was born in 1913 in North Carolina. He was the son of Matthew Monroe Almond and Minnie Lee Cooper. He married Margaret Shipes around 1933. Mack served his community as a City Policeman for the Charlotte Police Department. He passed away suddenly from a myocardial infarction in 1974 at Memorial Hospital in Charlotte. He is buried in Forest Lawn Cemetery.
