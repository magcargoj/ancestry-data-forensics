## Biography

Magnolia Catherine Tutor was born on May 30, 1883, in North Carolina. She was the daughter of Alfred Young Tutor (1852-1940) and Charlotte "Lottie" Honeycutt (1858-1941). She married Lonnie Frank Arnold around 1899. She appears in the 1910, 1920, and 1930 censuses in Buckhorn Township, Harnett County. She lived to be 89 years old, passing away on September 18, 1972. She is buried in the Baptist Grove Baptist Church Cemetery.
