## Biography

Magnolia "Maggie" Arnold was born on January 31, 1913, in Harnett County, North Carolina. She was the daughter of Lonnie Frank Arnold Sr. and Magnolia Catherine Tutor. She was named after her mother. She appears in the 1920 Census as a 7-year-old child in her parents' household in Buckhorn, Harnett County.

Maggie married John Perry Wood Sr. on July 4, 1929 (she was 17 in the 1930 census). She was a lifelong resident of North Carolina. She passed away on June 14, 1974, and is buried in the Wood Family Cemetery.
