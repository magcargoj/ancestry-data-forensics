## Biography

Margaret Robins was the daughter of Colonel Obedience Robins and his first wife, Margaret. She was first married to Dr. George Clark. After his death, she married [[William_Waters_Sr]] around 1650.

Margaret and William were step-siblings; after the death of William's father, his mother, Grace Neale, married Margaret's father, Obedience Robins. While some family traditions suggest they were first cousins, there is no verified biological evidence for this; they were primarily related by the marriage of their parents.
