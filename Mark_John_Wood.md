## Biography

Mark John Wood was born in 1834 in Cumberland County, NC. He was the son of **John Wood** and **Zilphia Goodman**. He married his first cousin, **Rebecca Smith Wood**. He served in the Civil War and lived in the Harnett/Cumberland area. He is buried in the Wood Family Cemetery.
