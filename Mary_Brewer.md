## Biography

Mary Brewer was born around 1827 in North Carolina. She married Calvin D. Brooks and was living in Ashe County in 1860. Following the death of her husband in the Civil War (1862), her son John Calvin Brooks was recorded living with his grandmother Phebe Brooks.
