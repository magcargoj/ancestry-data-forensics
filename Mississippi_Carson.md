## Biography

Mississippi Carson was born on June 30, 1858, in the historic community of Valle Crucis, Watauga County, North Carolina. Her parents, George and Sarah Carson, were among the established mountain families in the region. She was part of the generation that saw the building of the original Taylor (now Mast) General Store. She married Jesse John Holland Briley in 1880. Mississippi passed away on March 12, 1932, in Tarboro, Edgecombe County, North Carolina.
