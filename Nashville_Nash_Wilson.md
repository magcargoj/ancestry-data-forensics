## Biography

Nashville "Nash" Wilson was a farmer in the Swift Creek Township of Pitt County, North Carolina. He was a long-time resident of the area and his lands were recorded as adjoining other local prominent families. He married Drucilla Kite. He is believed to be buried in the Langley Cemetery in Pitt County.
