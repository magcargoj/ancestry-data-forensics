# Open Questions

Research gaps and unresolved questions, organized by priority and family line. Updated as questions are answered.

## High Priority (Would Significantly Advance Understanding)

### 1. Parentage of Oscar Rae Waters (1890-1965)
- **RESOLVED (2026-03-24)**: **James Que Waters** and **Mattie Thomas** confirmed as parents via `tree_data.json` and 1885 marriage consistency. The 1900 Census record listing William W. Waters was likely a misidentification or guardianship. Oscar's death date corrected to **19 Dec 1965**.

### 2. The "Waters Bridge" to Virginia/Maryland
- **The conflict**: Traditional trees place **Morgan William Waters** (b. 1660), **Cone Franklin Waters** (b. 1682), and **Johnny Dalton Waters** (b. 1700) as ancestors born in Bulloch County, Georgia. This is historically impossible as GA was founded in 1733.
- **Impact**: This bridge connects the Georgia/Florida branch to the documented Colonel William Waters Sr. line in Virginia.
- **Next step**: Trace the family of **Erastus Waters** (b. 1809) and **Milly Green** backward to North Carolina. Identify the specific Waters who married Milly Green.
- **Possible resolution**: The family likely migrated from NC to GA around 1800.
