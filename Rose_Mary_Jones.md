## Biography

Rose Mary Jones was born in 1923 in New Brighton, PA. She lived most of her life in Johnson City, TN. She was a former employee of Holiday Lanes and Leisure Lanes and was the first lady salesperson at Sherwood Chevrolet. She passed away in 2013.
