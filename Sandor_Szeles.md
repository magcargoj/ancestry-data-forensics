## Biography

Sandor (Alexander) Szeles was born around 1872 in Debraď, Moldava nad Bodvou, Slovakia. He was the son of Aloysius Szeles and Maria Farkas. He married Juliana (Julia) Szeles and they were the parents of Rozalia (Rose) Szelesova. Sandor passed away before 1930.
