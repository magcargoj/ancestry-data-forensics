## Biography

Thomas Gwaltney is the immigrant ancestor of the Gwaltney family in America. He arrived in Virginia in 1635 as an indentured servant but rose to become a landowner in Surry County. His original Welsh surname was likely Gwalchmai ("Hawk of the Plain").
