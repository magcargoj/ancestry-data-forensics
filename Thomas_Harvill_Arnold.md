## Biography

Thomas Harvill Arnold was born around 1830 in North Carolina. He was the son of **Harvill Thomas Arnold** and **Elizabeth Lanier**, who married in Cumberland County in 1829. He served in the Civil War and lived in the Johnsonville area of Harnett County. He married **Elizabeth "Betsy" Newell** on December 22, 1864, in Cumberland County.
