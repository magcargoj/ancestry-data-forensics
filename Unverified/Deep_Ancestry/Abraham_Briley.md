## Biography

Abraham Briley was a Revolutionary War veteran from Pitt County, North Carolina. He served in the NC Militia and was part of the early settlement of the county. In the early 19th century, he became part of the westward expansion, moving his family to Mississippi, where he died in 1831. His granddaughter, Mississippi Carson, was likely named in honor of this family connection.
