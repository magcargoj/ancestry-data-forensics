## Biography

Frederick Danner was a foundational pioneer of Watauga County and a veteran of the Revolutionary War. He was part of the early wave of settlers who moved into the Blue Ridge Mountains in the late 1700s. His descendants were key members of the Valle Crucis community, intermarrying with the Mast, Taylor, and Brooks families.
