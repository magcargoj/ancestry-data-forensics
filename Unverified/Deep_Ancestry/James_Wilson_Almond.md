## Biography

James Wilson Almond was born in 1860 in Stanly County, NC. He was the son of Green D. Almond and Lectie Jane Rogers. His father died in the Civil War when James was a young child. James became a prominent farmer in the county. He married Fannie Laura Scena Carter.
