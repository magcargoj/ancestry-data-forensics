## Biography

Jerry Mack Almond was born on February 22, 1934, in Charlotte, NC. He was the son of Mack (M.M.) Almond and Margaret Shipes. He married Madelyn Warner on June 20, 1953, in Mecklenburg County. He had a 45-year career in banking and was a prominent civic leader, serving as a Chatham County Commissioner. He passed away in 2025.
