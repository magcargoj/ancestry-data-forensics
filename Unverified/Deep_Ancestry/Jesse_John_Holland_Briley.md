## Biography

Jesse John Holland Briley was a member of the prominent Briley family of Pitt County, North Carolina. He lived in the Pactolus and Bethel areas and was a farmer. He married Mississippi Carson in 1880. His family has deep roots in the region, extending back to the Revolutionary War.
