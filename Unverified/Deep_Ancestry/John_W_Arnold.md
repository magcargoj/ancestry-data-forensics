## Biography

John W. Arnold was a farmer in the Barbecue and Upper Little River districts of Cumberland (later Harnett) County. He was born around 1820 and was an heir to the estate of his father, Solomon Arnold, in 1844. He resided with his mother, Mary, in the 1850 census.
