Margaret S. Shipes was born around 1911 in South Carolina. She married Mack Monroe Almond around 1933. She was the mother of Jerry Mack Almond. In 1940, she was living in Swain County, NC. She was still living in 1953 when her son Jerry married Madelyn Warner.
