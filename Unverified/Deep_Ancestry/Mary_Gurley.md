## Biography

Mary "Mollie" Gurley married Solomon Arnold on January 29, 1784, in Warren County, North Carolina. She moved with her husband to Moore and later Cumberland County. In 1845, as a widow, she applied for a federal pension based on her husband's Revolutionary War service, providing testimony about their marriage and life in North Carolina.
