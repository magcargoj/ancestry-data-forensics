## Biography

Michael D. Miller was a prominent resident of Ashe County, North Carolina. He was the son of David and Esther Miller. He lived in the North Fork/Horse Creek area. There is some conflicting information about his spouse in different records (Millie Wingler Bare vs. Patsy Blackburn), which requires further clarification, but his lineage from David Miller is well-established.
