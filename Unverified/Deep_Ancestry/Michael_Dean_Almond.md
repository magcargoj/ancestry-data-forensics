## Biography

Michael Dean Almond, known to his family as "Butch," was born in 1941. He was the son of Mack Monroe Almond and Margaret Shipes. He worked in the textile industry as a cutter. He tragically passed away in 1977 due to carbon monoxide asphyxiation during an apartment fire in Charlotte. He is buried in Forest Lawn Cemetery.
