## Biography

Richard Goodrich was born in Wethersfield, Connecticut in 1685. He was a resident of Glastonbury and later Middletown/Portland, CT. He married Hannah Bulkley in 1709. Richard was the grandson of the immigrant **William Goodrich** (c. 1631–1676), one of the first settlers of Wethersfield.
