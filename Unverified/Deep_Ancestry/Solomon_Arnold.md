## Biography

Solomon Arnold was a veteran of the Revolutionary War, serving as a private in the North Carolina Militia. He enlisted in Warren County and later lived in Moore and Cumberland counties. His estate was settled in Cumberland County in 1844. He is a primary ancestor of the Arnold family in the Harnett/Cumberland region.
