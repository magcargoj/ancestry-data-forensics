## Biography

Thomas Edwin Goodrich was born on January 9, 1876, in Sampson County, North Carolina. He was the son of Lewis Franklin Goodrich Sr. and Margaret Ann "Maggie" Culbreth. He married Mary Elvira "Mollie" Moye. He passed away on February 2, 1952, in Hassell, Martin County, North Carolina.
