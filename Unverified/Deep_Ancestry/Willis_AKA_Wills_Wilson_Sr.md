## Biography

Willis AKA Wills Wilson Sr. was born in 1752 in Craven, North Carolina (then British Colonial America). He married Lovey Elizabeth Wilson and they were the parents of Noah Nashville Wilson. Willis passed away on September 25, 1833, in Pitt County, North Carolina.
