## Biography

Virginia Dare Wilson Brown was born about 1923 in New Bern, North Carolina. She was the daughter of James Floyd Wilson and Cleo Marinda Goodrich. She was a career educator in Craven and Carteret counties. She passed away on January 19, 2017, in Atlantic Beach, North Carolina.
