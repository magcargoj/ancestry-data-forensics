## Biography

William B. "Joe" Moye was born in 1825 in Pitt County, North Carolina, the son of George Moye and Fereby Ferribee Pheribe Bell. He married Mary Elizabeth "Polly" Harris and they had a son, Macon Glasgow Moye. William served in the Civil War and passed away on August 30, 1865, in Louisiana.
