## Biography

William J. Arnold was born around 1850 in the area that became Harnett County, NC. He was a farmer in the Barbecue and Anderson Creek townships. He married Mary Elizabeth Matthews in the 1870s. The family was closely associated with the Barbecue Presbyterian Church community.
