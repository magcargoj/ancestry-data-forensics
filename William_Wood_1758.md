## Biography

William Wood (often referred to as William Wood 1758 to distinguish him from others) was born in 1758 in North Carolina. He was the son of Penuel Wood Sr. and Constance Nash. He was a Revolutionary War soldier, serving in the militia. He is the common ancestor of the Wood family in the Harnett/Cumberland area.
