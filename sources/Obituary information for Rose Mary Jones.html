Rose Mary Jones Obituary

And in the end-the love you get is equal to the love you give. BeatlesRose Mary Jones, 89, Johnson City, found peace at last, Monday, April 15, 2013 at NHC Healthcare, Johnson City, TN.Mrs. Jones was a native of New Brighton, PA and had lived most of her life in Johnson City.She was a daughter of the late Anthony and Rose Szeles Krupa and was also preceded in death by her husband, Clyde Jones, Jr, in 2011; two brothers, Tony and Emil Krupa and one sister, Ann Goff.Mrs. Jones was a former employee of Holiday Lanes and Leisure Lanes and was the first lady salesperson at Sherwood Chevrolet.She enjoyed listening to music, especially jitterbug dance music, books on tape, reading, traveling and the joy of her life was her family.Mrs. Jones leaves two daughters, Nancy Range and husband, Hal, Jonesborough; Dr. Sandra Waters and partner, Thomas Lowery, Fuquay-Varina, NC; two grandsons, Jason Waters, Santa Monica, CA and Josh Waters and wife, Melissa, Holly Springs, NC; great grandchildren, Ashley Werley, Connor Waters, Dylan Waters and Jeremy Woods; two sisters, Marge McKenzie, New Brighton, PA, Pat Claus, Phoenix, AZ ; one brother, Gene Krupa, New Brighton, PA; one sister-in-law, Thelma Bolinger, Sevierville, TN and many nieces and nephews.A special thank you to Roses family at NHC Healthcare Station 3 and Caris and special roommate Edith Stone.The family will receive friends from 12:30 PM Thursday, April 18, 2013 until a Celebration of Life Service at 1:15 PM at Tetrick Funeral and Cremation Services, Johnson City, TN. Interment services will follow at Mountain Home National Cemetery. Reverend Harold McNabb and Reverend Garland Thayer will officiate. Online condolences may be shared through www.tetrickfunealhome.com. Tetrick Funeral and Cremation Services, 3001 Peoples Street, Johnson City, TN 37604 (423)610-7171 is serving the family.
