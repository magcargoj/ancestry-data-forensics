Virginia Dare Wilson Brown

December 16, 1922 – January 19, 2017
IN THE CARE OF

Cotten Funeral Home

Virginia Dare Wilson Brown, 94, of Atlantic Beach, passed away Thursday, January 19, 2017 at home.

Virginia attended West New Bern Presbyterian Church and after moving to Atlantic Beach, she attended Bogue Banks Baptist Church. She taught in the North Carolina School System in Craven and Carteret counties until her retirement. Virginia was a charter member of Fidelis Delta Chapter of Alpha Delta Kappa, International Honorary Sorority for Women Educators. She is preceded in death by her husband Jesse Alton Brown, her son, James Marcus Brown, her parents, Floyd and Cleo Wilson, and two brothers, Cecil and Sam Wilson.

She is survived by her daughters, Diane Wood (John), Kathy Williams (Alvin) and Jennifer Robideaux, one sister, Claire Whitehurst, a sister in-law, Alice Wilson, 14 grandchildren and 31 great-grandchildren.

Visitation will be held at 10:00 am Saturday, January 28, 2017 at Cotten Funeral Home, followed by her funeral service at 11:00 am. Burial will follow at Greenleaf Memorial Park.

In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to Barium Springs Home for Children or Carteret County Home Health & Hospice.
